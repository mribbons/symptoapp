Sympto App
==========

Designed to assist health authorities with tracking infections

Plan
----
Users can enter symptom information for themselves and other family members
Users can also enter movement information for family members, or assign device GPS to their own device
Data, along with location is stored in cloud
Health authorities can detect emerging clusters by area using data above
Health authorities can then contact users to perform contact tracing
Users would register with either a phone number or email address for contact purposes

Public would be able to view aggregate information by area (Not specific GPS)
Public would receive updates and specific or general advice about emerging clusters
